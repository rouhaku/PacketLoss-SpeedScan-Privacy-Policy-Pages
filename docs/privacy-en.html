Privacy Policy

This app "PacketLoss & SpeedScan" may collect and use certain information to improve user experience and display advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

  • App usage data (launch count, error logs, etc.)
  • IP address (displayed for diagnostic purposes only and stored only on your device)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)

2. Purpose of Use

  • Analysis and display of network quality
  • Display of advertisements (using Google AdMob)
  • Crash and error analysis

3. External Data Transmission

This app uses the following external services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google User Messaging Platform (consent management)
  • SKAdNetwork (Apple's ad attribution)
  • Cloudflare (speed testing)

4. Tracking Permission

A tracking permission popup may appear when the app launches for advertising purposes. Personalized ads will not be displayed unless the user grants permission.

5. VPN Function and Packet Capture

This app uses a local VPN tunnel for network diagnostics. This VPN does not route through external servers, and all processing is completed within your device.

  • Captured packet data is stored only on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • When using the HTTPS decryption feature, a CA certificate is installed through user action

6. Subscriptions

This app offers in-app purchases (subscriptions). Payment processing is handled through Apple's App Store payment system, and we do not collect or store any payment information (such as credit card numbers).
