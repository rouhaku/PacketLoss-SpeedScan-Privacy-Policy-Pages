Privacy Policy

This app, "PacketLoss & SpeedScan", may collect and use certain information for the purposes below to improve the user experience and display advertisements.

1. Information Collected

  • App usage data (launch count, error logs, etc.)
  • IP address (displayed only for diagnostic purposes and stored only on the device)

2. Purpose of Use

  • Analyse and display communication quality
  • Display ads (using Google AdMob)
  • Analyse crashes and other issues

3. External Transmission of Information

This app uses the following external services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Cloudflare (speed diagnostics)

For displaying ads, a tracking permission pop-up may appear on app launch. Unless granted, personalised ads will not be displayed.
